## Quick Tips for Your LinkedIn Post:

1. **Best Time to Post**: 
   - Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM or 12-2 PM IST
   - Avoid weekends for professional content

2. **Add a Cover Image**: 
   - Screenshot of your portfolio homepage
   - Use a tool like Canva to add text overlay
   - Recommended size: 1200x627px

3. **Engage with Comments**:
   - Respond to every comment within 2 hours
   - Ask follow-up questions
   - Thank people for their feedback

4. **Tag Relevant People**:
   - Mentors who helped you
   - Colleagues who gave feedback
   - Companies you'd like to work for (subtly)

5. **Follow-up Posts**:
   - Day 2: Share a behind-the-scenes video
   - Day 4: Post about a specific feature
   - Week 2: Share lessons learned

6. **Use LinkedIn Features**:
   - Add document carousel with screenshots
   - Create a short video walkthrough
   - Use LinkedIn polls to engage audience
